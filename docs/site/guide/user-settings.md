# User Settings & Personalization

Learn how to manage your account details and customize your visual experience in All Time Wrestling RPG.

## Profile Drawer

Access your personal settings by clicking the **Profile** button in the top navigation bar. This opens a dedicated drawer on the right side of the screen, allowing you to manage your account without leaving your current context.

![Profile Drawer](/screenshots/profile-drawer.png)

### Managing Account Details

*   **Username:** Your username is read-only and cannot be changed.
*   **Email:** Update your email address for notifications and account recovery.
*   **Password:** Click **Change Password** to securely update your credentials. You will need to provide your current password for verification.

## On-Demand Themes

Customize the look and feel of the application to suit your preference or lighting conditions. The selected theme is saved to your user profile and will persist across different sessions and devices.

### Available Themes

*   **Light:** The default clean, bright interface.
*   **Dark:** A modern dark mode, easy on the eyes for low-light environments.
*   **Retro:** A sepia-toned, nostalgic aesthetic reminiscent of old wrestling posters.
*   **Neon:** A high-contrast, vibrant cyberpunk-inspired theme.
*   **High Contrast:** A strictly black-and-white theme for maximum readability and accessibility.

### How to Change Your Theme

1.  Open the **Profile** drawer.
2.  Locate the **Theme** dropdown.
3.  Select your desired theme.
4.  Click **Save Changes**.
5.  The application will reload to apply the new visual style globally.
