# Community

Welcome to the Community guide. This documentation is automatically generated from the latest game features.

## Social Media Export

Share your show cards with the world! The Social Media Export tool generates perfectly formatted match cards for Markdown, Facebook, X (Twitter), and Bluesky. Choose to include match results, segment summaries, and highlighted main events to keep your community engaged and informed.

### Managing Faction Members

Wrestler added to the faction roster — the members grid updates instantly. Members can be removed at any time without affecting their match or feud history; faction membership is independent of past results.
