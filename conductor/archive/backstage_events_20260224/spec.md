# Interactive Backstage "Dialogue Tree" Events - Specification

## Overview

Introduce random backstage encounters in Campaign Mode. A player might be approached by a rival, potential manager, or reporter, with choices affecting attributes and alignment.

## Features

- **Random Encounters:** Triggered between matches or segments.
- **Dialogue Trees:** AI-generated conversations with multiple choice responses.
- **Attribute Impact:** Choices affect Charisma, Drive, or Face/Heel alignment.
- **Hidden Feuds:** Interactions can spark rivalries outside of matches.
