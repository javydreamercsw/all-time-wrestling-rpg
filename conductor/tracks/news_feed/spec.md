# AI-Generated "Wrestling World" News Feed - Specification

## Overview

Add a real-time news ticker or "Social Media" dashboard that synthesizes recent match results, title changes, and backstage rumors into a living feed.

## Features

- **News Ticker:** Scrolling feed of headlines.
- **Social Media Dashboard:** Mock social posts from wrestlers and fans.
- **AI Synthesis:** Generate news items based on actual game events.
- **Rumor Mill:** Procedural generation of trade rumors or injury reports.
