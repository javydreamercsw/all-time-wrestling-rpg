# Persistent "Wear & Tear" & Career Longevity - Specification

## Overview

Implement a hidden "Physical Condition" stat that tracks cumulative damage from high-risk matches. As this stat degrades, wrestlers face longer recovery or permanent penalties.

## Features

- **Physical Condition Stat:** Hidden tracker for cumulative damage.
- **Match Impact:** High-risk matches (Cage, TLC) degrade condition faster.
- **Injury Thresholds:** Low condition increases injury probability.
- **Retirement Storyline:** AI-scripted storylines for wrestlers nearing retirement.
