# Enhanced "Hall of Fame" & Career Retrospectives - Specification

## Overview

Expand the Hall of Fame into a comprehensive "Legacy Dashboard." It features interactive career stats, a "Rivalry Heat Map," and AI-narrated career retrospectives.

## Features

- **Legacy Dashboard:** Comprehensive view of career achievements.
- **Rivalry Heat Map:** Visualizing key opponents and feud intensity.
- **AI Retrospective:** Generated biography upon retirement.
- **Hall of Fame Criteria:** Dynamic tracking of eligibility (Titles, Wins, Fans).
